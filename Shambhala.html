Shambhala Mountain Center, Colorado, USA

Tailored Experiences: Each destination is chosen to provide the ultimate relaxation and meditation experience.

Set in the stunning Rocky Mountains, Shambhala Mountain Center offers a unique blend of natural beauty and spiritual practice. The 600-acre retreat provides ample space for meditation, yoga, and nature-based programs. The majestic mountain views and serene environment make it an ideal destination for a holiday retreat focused on mindfulness and relaxation. During the holidays, Shambhala Mountain Center’s tranquil setting allows you to escape the seasonal rush and find inner peace. The center’s programs, which include mindfulness-based stress reduction and personal retreats, are designed to help you unwind and rejuvenate. Whether you’re meditating in the Great Stupa or hiking through the scenic trails, Shambhala offers a peaceful and restorative holiday experience.
